## Why this list

User asked for law firms similar to Prior Law (which uses external CTO / outsourced legal-SaaS). The targeting rationale:
- **Already-buyer signals** matter most: KPA (Justly), Prior Law (LEAP+LawConnect), O'Dor (PEXA) have proven they pay for outsourced tech. Cold outreach about additional automation has a higher hit rate with firms already in the buying habit.
- **Size band** (~5-22 lawyers): big enough to feel the admin pain, small enough that the principal will read their own email.
- **Skipped top-tier** (Allens, HSF, MinterEllison, Clayton Utz, Ashurst, KWM, Norton Rose, Gilbert+Tobin, Arnold Bloch Leibler, Holding Redlich): these have in-house IT departments.
- **Skipped 4 firms** (Velocity Legal, Sladen Legal, KHQ, Avery Commercial) where I couldn't find a non-generic finding within the time budget; rather than pad, returned fewer high-quality rows.
