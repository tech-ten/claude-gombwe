When we’re not the right fit

We’ll tell you when this isn’t the right fit.

Some work is genuinely person-shaped, not process-shaped. Sales relationships, on-site trades, clinical care, team leadership. We’ll say so on the first call.

Where we’re a good fit is steady, repetitive, software-shaped work. The 20% that’s real judgment, we leave with you. The 80% that’s clicks, copy-paste, and reminders, we take.
