What it looks like for your staff

Same Claude app on their phone.
Now answering from your business.

We connect the AI app your team already uses to your CRM, calendar, accounting and inbox. They ask questions in plain English. The AI answers from your real data, in real time.

Reception

“Who hasn’t confirmed for tomorrow? Send them an SMS reminder, friendly tone, mention parking is free out front.”

AI pulls tomorrow’s diary, identifies the unconfirmed patients, drafts the SMS in your practice voice, sends after reception taps approve. Two minutes of work instead of 20.

Bookkeeper

“What did we spend with Henry Schein last quarter? Pull the invoices and summarise for the BAS.”

AI queries Xero, returns the total, breaks it down by month, and drafts the journal note in the format the bookkeeper uses. No CSV exports, no pivot tables, no spreadsheet juggling.

Practice owner

“Which patients haven’t been in for 18 months and still have private health? Draft the recall email.”

AI cross-references the practice management system with the recall rules you set, drafts a personalised email per patient referencing their last treatment, and queues them for your sign-off.

How it works

Three steps.
Two to four weeks.

Week 1

Audit

We map your existing stack: CRM, calendar, accounting, comms. We work out which questions your team asks most and what data the AI needs access to. Fixed fee, no commitment beyond.

Weeks 2-3

Build

We build the integration between your systems and Claude or ChatGPT (whichever your team prefers). All data stays in Australian regions where the source systems already live. No data goes to anyone outside your business.

One session with your team showing the questions that work best. From then on, ongoing monitoring and prompt refinement from us. Your team learns more questions as they go.

What this is NOT

We’ll be clear about what we’re not building.

This is not a customer-facing chatbot. Strangers cannot talk to your data through this integration. It is internal only.

This is not a replacement for your CRM or your accounting system. Those systems stay. The AI becomes a faster way for your team to ask them questions.

This is not a feature you have to learn a new app for. Your team uses the Claude or ChatGPT app they may already have on their phone. The integration is invisible from their side.
