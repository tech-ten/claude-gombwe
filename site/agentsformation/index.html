How it works

Three steps.
Audit, stabilise, automate.

You don’t need to know which bits are IT and which bits are automation. We walk through your operations, fix what’s shaky, then remove the highest-friction admin job each month.

Step 1

Audit

A one-day walk-through of your business. Every spreadsheet, every manual handoff, every IT issue you’ve been working around. We leave with a written list, ranked by hours saved.

Step 2

Stabilise

Fix the IT foundation first. Nothing else works on broken Wi-Fi or a half-configured M365 tenant. Helpdesk goes live. Backups get tested. Security gets sane defaults.

Step 3

Automate, one job at a time

Every month, we pick the next highest-friction admin task from the audit list and remove it. You see the hours saved on the next invoice. We don’t sell “an AI platform.” We sell hours back to your team, one specific automation at a time.
