# Meeting Prep

Check the calendar for the next upcoming meeting (or the one the user specifies) and prepare:

1. **Meeting context** — what is this meeting about, who called it, is it recurring?
2. **Attendees** — who will be there, what are their roles, any recent interactions?
3. **Previous meeting notes** — if this is a recurring meeting, what was discussed last time?
4. **Prep checklist:**
   - Documents or data to review beforehand
   - Questions you should be ready to answer
   - Topics you might want to raise
5. **Talking points** — 3-5 bullet points for your contribution
6. **Time check** — how long until the meeting, flag if you need to prepare anything urgent

Keep it focused and actionable. Don't pad with generic advice.
